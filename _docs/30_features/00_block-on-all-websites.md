If you want to block all your visits on all the websites that have Simple Analytics installed. Our own extension does not allow this we don't want access to all your websites that you browse. But if you want to block your visits on all, there is a way.

1. [Download Site Blocker](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/site-blocker/npfefgkhblhhgcngndgecbeoaabeclee/related) on the Chrome web store.
1. Give the extsion access to all your websites

    <img class="border" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/site-blocker-01-install-extension.png" alt="">

1. Click the extensions icon
    
    <img class="border" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/site-blocker-02-open-settings.png" alt="">
    
1. And click on Site Blocker

1. A modal appears. Fill the URL in you want to block. Probably `https://scripts.simpleanalyticscdn.com/latest.js`

    <img class="border" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/site-blocker-03-add-simple-analytics.png" alt="">

1. Select "Block exact URL"
1. Click "Block site"

Now every our script is blocked on every website you visit.
